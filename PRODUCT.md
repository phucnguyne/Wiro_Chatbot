# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users
General users seeking an all-purpose conversational AI assistant to assist with tasks such as writing, coding, brainstorming, and answering general questions.

## Product Purpose
Wiro Chatbot is a full-screen, chat-first conversational assistant that aims to provide an intuitive, smooth, and engaging interaction space. It bridges simple utility commands (dice, coin, time) with advanced, general-purpose Gemini AI responses.

## Positioning
An effortless conversational companion where the interface fades into the background so you can type, send, and receive clear, beautifully typeset responses naturally.

## Brand Personality
Sleek, minimalist, and tech-forward, combined with a friendly, playful undertone. It communicates through clean layouts, subtle borders, and fast, smooth transitions.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, widget-heavy templates or busy sidebars that distract from the main conversation.
- Generic ChatGPT clones using cheap boxy input fields and dull, uninspired gray message blocks.
- Low-contrast layouts with hard-to-read fonts or aggressive, distracting animations.

## Design Principles
- **Chat-First Focus**: Every UI element is designed to keep the user in the conversational flow, avoiding widgets or secondary items that clutter the screen.
- **Sleek yet Playful**: Balance clean, tech-forward alignment with subtle playful transitions, satisfying button clicks, and friendly colors.
- **Flawless Readability**: Prioritize typography, line lengths, and message bubble spacing so reading and writing feel effortless and natural.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict adherence to WCAG AA contrast standards, ensuring body text remains highly readable.
- Native support for both light and dark mode preferences with a clean toggle interface.
- Respect for reduced motion preferences (`prefers-reduced-motion`) by substituting transitions with instant or fade effects.
